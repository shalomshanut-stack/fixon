Academics

Cedarston International Christian School offers a blended academic pathway that supports students from the early grades through graduation. Our program combines the structure of Accelerated Christian Education with the internationally recognized Ontario Secondary School Diploma pathway.

From Grade 1 to Grade 9, students follow the ACE program, building strong foundations in core subjects, independent study, discipline, and Christian character. From Grade 10 to Grade 12, learners transition into the OSSD program, preparing them for university, future careers, and life beyond school.

Our Academic Pathway

Grade 1 - 9

ACE Program

Students build strong academic foundations through the Accelerated Christian Education program, developing responsibility, independent learning habits, subject mastery, and Christian character.

Grade 10 - 12

OSSD Program

Senior students complete the Ontario Secondary School Diploma pathway, gaining internationally recognized preparation for university, career opportunities, and confident graduation.

Graduation

Ready For The Future

By graduation, students are equipped with academic knowledge, practical skills, strong values, and the confidence to continue learning, leading, and serving.
