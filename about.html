About Us

Cedarston International Christian School is a learning community committed to academic excellence, Christian values, and character development. We help students grow in knowledge, confidence, and purpose as they prepare for the future.

Our mission is to equip students with the knowledge, skills, and character to succeed in a rapidly changing world. We provide a rigorous academic program, a supportive community, and opportunities for leadership and service.
